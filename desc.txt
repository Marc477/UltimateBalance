Improves some skills, spells and creatures that are too weak in the original game, to increase the amount of useful options. This mod does not nerf anything.

    

Spells: 

Forturne / Misfortune / Mirth / Sorrow:
Now affect all units in a zone. Zone gets bigger the higher the magic school level. Expert level still affects all units. Gives an extra +1/-1 luck or morale.

Protection from Air/Water/Fire/Earth:
Now affect all units in a zone. Zone gets bigger the higher the magic school level. Expert level still affects all units.

Magic Mirror: 
Percentage to reflect the spell is increased from 20/30/40% to 50/50/50%. Targets all units in a zone that gets bigger with higher magic school level, expert level affects all units. Mana cost is reduced.

Precision / Prayer: 
The amount of attack (and defense for prayer) has been increased by an extra +1.

Disrupting Ray:
Defense changed from -3/-4/-5 to -3/-5/-7.

Slayer:
Bonus Attack against specified creatures, increased from 8/8/8 to 8/12/16

Fire Shield:
Damage dealt increased from 20/25/30% to 20/30/40%

Frost Ring / Fireball / Inferno / Land Mine:
Damage is increased to 15 per power instead of 10 per power.

Cure:
Healing increased from 5 to 7 per power.

Summon Boat / Scuttle Boat / Countestrike / Earthquake / Sacrifice / Clone:
Cost less mana point to cast

    
Skills: 

Eagle Eye:
Chances to learn a spell increased from 40/50/60% to 50/65/80%
Spell level that can be learned increased from 2/3/4 to 3/4/5

First Aid:
Extra Healing increased from 50/75/100   to 75/150/250

Artillery:
All levels of this skill now have 100% chance to do x2 damage.
Balista will shoot 1/2/3 arrow based on skill level.

Learning:
Experienced gained increased from 5/10/15% to 10/20/30%

Sorcery:
Spell damage increased from 5/10/15% to 10/20/30%


    
Creatures:

Imps / Familiars
Cost reduced from 50/60 to 40/50

Gogs / Magogs
Cost reduced from 125/175 to 110/160

Orcs / Orcs Chieftains
Weekly growth increased by 1

Cyclops Kings
Cost reduced from 1100 to 1000, Unupgraded cyclops cost is unchanged

Hydra / Chaos Hydra
Attack and Defense increased by 1

Tower Town Mage Tower
Does not require Golem Factory anymore to be built.
